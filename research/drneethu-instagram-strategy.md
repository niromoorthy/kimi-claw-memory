## 3. Content Strategy

### 3.1 Content Pillars (80/20 Rule)

| Pillar | % | Content Types |
|--------|---|---------------|
| **Health Education** | 40% | Myth-busting, symptom explanations, prevention tips |
| **Behind the Scenes** | 20% | Day in life, clinic moments, personal wellness |
| **Patient Stories** | 15% | Anonymized cases, common concerns, FAQs |
| **Lifestyle/Motherhood** | 15% | Being a doctor mom, work-life balance |
| **StudyMed Synergy** | 10% | Medical career advice, supporting aspiring doctors |

### 3.2 Content Formats

**Reels (Priority):**
- 15-30 seconds
- Trending audio + health twist
- "3 signs you need to see a GP"
- "What I eat in a day as a doctor"
- "POV: You're my patient with..."

**Carousels:**
- "5 things your GP wishes you knew"
- "Understanding blood tests"
- "Women's health checklist by age"

**Stories (Daily):**
- Polls: "Have you had your BP checked this year?"
- Q&A: "Ask me anything about..."
- Day-in-life: Clinic, home, family
- Reposts: Share relevant health news

**Static Posts:**
- Quote cards
- Infographics
- Personal reflections

### 3.3 Content Calendar (Weekly)

| Day | Content |
|-----|---------|
| Monday | Educational Reel |
| Tuesday | Carousel post |
| Wednesday | Behind-the-scenes Stories |
| Thursday | Educational Reel |
| Friday | Personal/lifestyle post |
| Saturday | Q&A Stories + patient FAQ |
| Sunday | Planning + community engagement |

## 4. Growth Strategy

### 4.1 First 1,000 Followers (Month 1-2)

**Tactics:**
- Post daily (mix of formats)
- Engage with 50 accounts/day (other doctors, health pages, local community)
- Use 10-15 relevant hashtags
- Collaborate with micro-influencers
- Share to Stories consistently

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- **Broad:** #gp #doctor #womenshealth #healthtips
- **Niche:** #australiandoctor #melbournegp #womenshealthmatters
- **Community:** #doctorsoninstagram #medicalblogger #healthblogger
- **Personal:** #doctorlife #medicine #healthcare

### 4.2 1,000 to 10,000 Followers (Month 3-6)

**Tactics:**
- Double down on Reels (algorithm favor)
- Start series: "GP Explains" weekly
- Guest appearances on other health accounts
- Local PR: "Melbourne GP taking health education to Instagram"
- Patient testimonials (with permission)

### 4.3 10,000+ Followers (Month 6-12)

**Tactics:**
- Brand partnerships (health products, wellness brands)
- Speaking opportunities
- Media features
- Online consultations
- Course/membership launch
