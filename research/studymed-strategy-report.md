## 1. Executive Summary

StudyMed has achieved a critical milestone: **first student admission into TMA** with exclusive Aus/NZ rights. This is a strong proof point. Now the focus shifts to scaling admissions and establishing market dominance in the medical admission consulting space.

**Key Opportunity:** Medical school admissions consulting is a high-value, high-trust market with significant barriers to entry. StudyMed's exclusive TMA rights provide a unique competitive moat.

## 2. Competitive Landscape Analysis

### 2.1 Direct Competitors (Medical Admission Focus)

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | StudyMed Advantage |
|------------|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| **MedView Education** | Largest in Australasia, UCAT tutoring, brand recognition | Expensive, less personalized, no exclusive partnerships | Exclusive TMA rights, more personalized |
| **MedEntry** | Strong UCAT platform, discount codes, market leader | Focused on UCAT only, less holistic | End-to-end admission support |
| **QConsult** | UK/Singapore/Australia focus, doctor mentors | Smaller brand, less digital presence | Exclusive rights, better digital marketing |
| **Ngo & Sons** | Director is UNSW Medicine grad, strong reputation | Sydney-focused, limited scale | National reach, exclusive partnerships |
| **J&J Education** | Multi-award winning, US/UK focus | Not medical-specific | Medical-only focus, specialist expertise |

### 2.2 Indirect Competitors

- **General college counselors** (InGenius Prep, Lygon Group) — not medical-specific
- **University career centers** — free but limited
- **Peer tutoring** — cheap but unprofessional

### 2.3 Market Gap Analysis

**Gap 1: Exclusive Partnerships**
- No competitor has exclusive rights to specific medical schools
- StudyMed's TMA exclusive is unique in market

**Gap 2: Personalized High-Touch Service**
- Large competitors (MedView, MedEntry) are platform-heavy
- Opportunity for white-glove, boutique service

**Gap 3: International Student Focus**
- TMA specifically targets international pathways
- Huge market in India, SE Asia, Middle East
